Game rules
What is Dara?

Dara is a two-player strategy board game played in several countries of West Africa

How to play
  • In the beginning of the game, the board is empty
  • Players take turns placing their tokens in the empty cells
  • Be careful, you cannot make a three-in-a-row yet
  • Once both players have placed their 12 tokens, the move phase begins
  • In the move phase your mission is to get 3 of your tokens in a row
  • Diagonals do not count
  • You cannot make more than three-in-a-row (4 or 5 are not allowed)
  • Once you get a three-in-a-row you may remove one of your opponents tokens
  • Whoever cannot make a three-in-a-row anymore i.e has only 2 tokens left, loses
Good luck and play fair!
